Description

Just two years old and immaculately presented, this stunning four-bedroom contemporary residence offers a fresh take on Grey Lynn living in the heart of the sought-after heritage suburb.

Flowing intuitively across two spacious floors, at ground level are three generous double bedrooms, flooded with natural light and sharing a family bathroom with bath and cosy living room that kids will love to claim as their own. Doors lead directly out to low maintenance alfresco living spaces and the pool, all private and secure for little ones and pets and just waiting to be enjoyed once the warmer months roll around. Also on this level is a well-appointed laundry, and internal access to garaging and off street for an additional vehicle.

On the upper level is the sun soaked primary bedroom, with walk in robe and en suite with separate powder room thoughtfully included. Open plan living and dining have been designed to harness natural light and make the most of space, with a gabled roof and neutral palette onto which to stamp your style. The kitchen is a study in cool Scandi style, with pale wood cabinetry and sprawling island for guests to perch at while prep for dinner. Living flows directly out to alfresco living with Louvretec roof included given Aucklands occasionally unpredictable weather, and an office nook is also in place for those lucky enough to work from home.

Just steps from the cosmopolitan cool of West Lynn Village and its great restaurants, bars and independent retailers and within walking distance of Western Springs and Grey Lynn parks, this home is perfectly located to soak up all the popular city fringe neighbourhood has to offer. The dream low maintenance alternative to villa living and very easy to love, make your appointment to view this fabulous home today.
